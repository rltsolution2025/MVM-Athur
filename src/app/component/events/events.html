Celebrating Independence Day with Pride and Patriotism

At Maharishi Vidya Mandir School, Independence Day is celebrated with great enthusiasm, honoring the spirit of freedom and unity. The day begins with the hoisting of the national flag, followed by the soulful rendition of the national anthem, filling every heart with pride and patriotism.

Students participate in inspiring speeches, patriotic songs, cultural performances, and skits that reflect India’s rich heritage and the sacrifices made by our freedom fighters. The vibrant atmosphere instills in students the values of responsibility, unity, and respect for the nation.

The celebration not only reminds us of the struggles of the past but also encourages our young learners to dream big and contribute positively towards building a stronger and brighter India.

At Maharishi Vidya Mandir, Athur, Chengalpattu, Independence Day is a moment of reflection, pride, and inspiration, nurturing responsible citizens of tomorrow.
